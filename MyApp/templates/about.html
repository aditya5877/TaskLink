About TaskLink

Connecting Skilled Workers with Opportunities

Who We Are

TaskLink is an innovative online job portal designed to bridge the gap between employers and skilled workers. Whether you're an employer seeking reliable help for tasks like painting, cleaning, security, or driving, or a worker looking for opportunities that match your skillset, TaskLink is here to make the process fast, simple, and effective. Launched in 2025, we are committed to empowering communities by creating meaningful connections in the job market.

Our Services

For Employers

Employers can easily find skilled workers for a variety of tasks. Subscribe to TaskLink, browse worker profiles, and hire reliable help for jobs like painting, cleaning, security, driving, and more—all in just a few clicks.

For Workers

Workers can register on TaskLink to showcase their skills and find tasks that suit their expertise. Whether you’re a painter, driver, or cleaner, TaskLink connects you with employers who need your talents.

Why Choose TaskLink?

  • Fast & Efficient: Find workers or jobs in minutes, not days.
  • Reliable Connections: Verified profiles ensure trust and quality.
  • Wide Variety: From painting to security, we cover all kinds of skills.
  • Local Focus: Serving Pune and Mumbai, we connect you with nearby opportunities.
  • Simple Process: Easy registration and subscription for seamless access.

Where We Work

TaskLink proudly serves the bustling cities of Pune and Mumbai, helping employers and workers connect locally.
